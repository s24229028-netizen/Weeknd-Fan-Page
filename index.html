ABEL TESFAYE

Born in Toronto, Abel Makkonen Tesfaye rose from mystery to become one of music's most influential voices. The Weeknd's dark R&B aesthetic and cinematic storytelling have redefined contemporary music.

From the underground mixtape trilogy to chart-topping albums like "Starboy," "After Hours," and "Dawn FM," his evolution showcases artistic fearlessness and innovation.

With multiple Grammy Awards, billions of streams, and a signature sound that blends 80s nostalgia with futuristic production, The Weeknd continues to push boundaries.
